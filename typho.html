Typhoid Awareness

What is Typhoid?

Typhoid is a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella typhi. It spreads through contaminated food and water, especially in areas with poor sanitation.

Causes

  • Drinking contaminated water
  • Eating food handled by infected persons
  • Poor hygiene and sanitation

Symptoms

  • Prolonged high fever
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Stomach pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Headache

Prevention

  • Drink safe, boiled or purified water
  • Wash hands before eating
  • Avoid street food or unhygienic food
  • Get vaccinated if needed

Treatment

Typhoid is treated with antibiotics prescribed by a doctor. It's important to complete the full course of medication. Drink plenty of fluids and take rest.
